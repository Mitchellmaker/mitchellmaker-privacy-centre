Twigged Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 7 July 2026

Overview

Twigged respects your privacy. This application is designed to help users remember where they have stored everyday items. Twigged does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

Twigged may store information entered by the user, such as item names and the places where those items have been saved.

If the user chooses to use voice input, Apple Speech Recognition is used to convert spoken requests into text.

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to provide the functionality of the application, including saving item locations, finding saved items, and answering natural language requests.

Data Storage

Saved item information is stored locally on the user's device using Apple's local storage technologies. Twigged does not require an account and does not transmit saved item information to MitchellMaker servers.

Speech Recognition

Speech Recognition is used only when the user chooses to speak to the app. Spoken requests are converted into text so Twigged can understand whether the user wants to save or find an item.

Third-Party Services

Twigged does not sell personal information and does not use advertising networks.

Offline Use

Once information has been saved, Twigged can continue to find saved items without an internet connection.

Your Rights

You may delete saved items inside the app at any time. You may also remove all saved information by deleting the application from your device.
