MemoryQ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13 June 2026

Overview

MemoryQ respects your privacy. This application is designed to help users store and recall important information using photos, voice recordings and text notes. MemoryQ does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

The app may access:

  • Photos selected or captured by the user
  • Voice recordings created by the user
  • Text entered by the user

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to provide the functionality of the application.

Data Storage

All memories, photos, recordings and notes are stored locally on the user's device unless the user chooses to share or export them.

Third-Party Services

MemoryQ does not sell personal information and does not use advertising networks.

Your Rights

You may delete your stored information at any time by deleting memories within the app or removing the application from your device.
