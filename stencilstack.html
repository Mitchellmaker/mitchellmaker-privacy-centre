StencilStack Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 13 June 2026

Overview

StencilStack respects your privacy. This application is designed to help users create multi-layer stencil artwork from photographs and images and does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

The app may access:

  • Photos selected by the user
  • Files created by the user within the app

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to provide the functionality of the application.

Data Storage

All user content is stored locally on the user's device unless the user chooses to export or share it.

Third-Party Services

StencilStack does not sell personal information and does not use advertising networks.

Your Rights

You may delete your content at any time by removing it from your device or deleting the application.
