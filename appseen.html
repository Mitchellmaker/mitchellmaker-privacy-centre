AppSeen Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

AppSeen respects your privacy. AppSeen is designed to help indie developers organise and share promotional content for their apps. The app does not sell, rent or share personal information with third parties.

Information We Collect

The app may access:

  • Text shared by the user
  • Images selected by the user
  • Videos selected by the user
  • App information created by the user
  • Files created within the app

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to provide the functionality of the application.

Content remains under the user's control and is used only for creating, organising and sharing promotional posts.

Data Sharing

AppSeen does not sell, rent or share personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Content is stored locally on the user's device unless the user chooses to share it using Apple's standard sharing features.
